Why this matters

Cybersecurity is not only about technology. It is about helping everyday people understand how phishing, social engineering, and credential theft can target them directly in ordinary online life.

People are often the target

Many successful attacks focus on individuals rather than systems, using tricks and pressure to exploit weak habits.

Safer habits protect more than one account

When people use better online habits, they reduce the risk of losing money, privacy, identity, and access to important services.

Education makes a real difference

Learning practical cyber safety helps people build long-lasting habits that contribute to a safer digital community for everyone.

What makes CyberTrail stand out?

Interactive simulations

Branching stories
